The Best Movies According To Rik

My Top Three Movies of all-time.

Grave of The Fireflies

This is one of my favourite animes, it's about a post war situation of a brother and a sister having their parents killed in the war and them being neglected by the remaining of their family.

Wall-e

This is a cartoon movie. It is based on a futuristic approach of post apocalyptic earth where robots are the sole survivors who come visit earth and one of the female robots fall in love with a stranded male ugly robot who was living on his accord on earth. The movie is cute.

Jumanji

I really loved this movie back when I was a kid. Its about a board game where things actually come to life with the instructions passed on by the board.
